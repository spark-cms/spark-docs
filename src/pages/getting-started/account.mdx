# Creating an Account
import { Steps } from 'nextra/components'

<Steps>
### Go to the registration page
Go to the [registration page](https://spark-cms.io/register) and enter your email address, username and password. Then click the "Register" button.

### Confirm your account
After you've clicked the "Register" button, you will be sent an email with a link to confirm your account. Click the link in the email to confirm your account.
Alternatively, you can copy the confirmation code from the email and paste it into the confirmation code field on the confirmation page you were redirected to 
after registering.
</Steps>
